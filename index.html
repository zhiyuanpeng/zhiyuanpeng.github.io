Bio

Hi! I'm a final-year PhD student in Computer Science and Engineering at Santa Clara University, working under the guidance of Professor Yi Fang, where I studied enhancing search systems with large language models, including query understanding, retrieval and reranking. Prior to that, I got my M.S. degree in Computer Science and engineering from Santa Clara University in 2020.
